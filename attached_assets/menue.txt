## Notes & Observations
- **GST**: 5% additional applies across every section of the menu.
- **Veg/Non-veg dual pricing**: several sections (pasta, salads, hot pot) list two prices — the lower is typically Veg, the higher Non-veg.
- **Veg icon inconsistencies**: the small square veg-symbol appears next to a couple of chicken items (Peri Peri Chicken pizza, The Savor Bhutanese) — likely menu-design errors worth flagging to the restaurant before printing a final version.
- **Combos**: Burger Combos come with chicken wings, coleslaw, fries, AND a lemon mint mohito — a stronger value bundle than the "All Time Bistro Favourite" burgers, which include wings/nuggets, coleslaw and fries but no drink.
- Some handwritten items are stylistically named (e.g., "Country Roads Take Me Home," "Wowy Waffles," "Heard A Lot Peri-Peri Chicken") — consider standardizing naming if this is going into a POS or online ordering system.
